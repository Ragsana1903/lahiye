Do you offer payment plans?

I sure do! I do require a 40% deposit to hold your spot but I am happy to work out a payment plan with you for the remainder of the project.

Do you teach me how to edit my website?

Heck yes! The reason I design and code in Squarespace is because it is so user-friendly. My goal is to empower you to be able to edit everything on your own. Once we launch your site, we will schedule an hour long call (or so) to go over how to use Squarespace, how to edit everything on your site, what you need to touch and what you don’t and ask answer any questions you might have.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

My goal is to empower all of my clients with the ability to edit their website and content easily, on their own, so that you are free of the monthly retainer! However, if you are in a bind and need some extra help, want to add pages or have new products or services launching, I am always here. I do offer hourly, half-day and full-day rates for website edits.

What if I don't need branding or a website. Do you take on other design work?
